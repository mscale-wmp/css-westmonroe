Who We Are

We are the developers that love waterfalls. No, not waterfall vs agile, but everflowing cascade of styling websites. We are graphic designs, accesibility enforcers, color theory arguers, and much, much, more.

What is CSS?

Cascading Style Sheets, CSS, is what gives the internet technicolor beauty. Every website, every web app uses CSS. When you hover over a button and the color changes, that is CSS at work. This pod focuses on CSS3, SCSS/Sass, and much more.
